One multi-industry token solution

  • Gaming & NFTs

    Boost game engagement and player retention. Offer branded in-game coins to incentivize existing players and entice new ones by letting them redeem coins for rewards.

    · Launch your own branded token for your game ecosystem, and even additional nested branded tokens for each game, experience or even for players.

    · Use tokens to reward ideal behaviors from new users and existing players who engage and participate in games.

    · Let users redeem tokens for in-game upgrades and access to future experiences or redeem the coin for financial upside.

  • Metaverse

    Launch your own metaverse token. Enable all of your citizens to earn, buy, redeem, sell, and accrue your branded token. Incentivize a whole new virtual experience.

    · Launch your own branded token for your Metaverse experience or a set of nested tokens for each unique experience or venue.

    · Allow citizens to use the token to purchase digital assets, skins, property or to redeem for access to unique events and experiences.

    · Empower a thriving economy in your ecosystem, where goods and services are bought and traded using your branded coin.

  • Digital markteting

    Gift tokens to customers to entice them into the engagement funnel and reward them via game mechanics, such as learn-to-earn and engage-to-earn. Provide tokens for conversion, purchase, promotions or special perks.

    · Entice your prospects closer to conversion by launching your own branded token, which they can earn, purchase or receive.

    · Build game mechanics that drive user engagement, such as engage-to-earn or learn-to-earn programs where prospects get rewarded for interacting.

    · Offer perks for holding the token, such as access to events, communities or content, and a unique menu to redeem tokens for goods, services or experiences.

  • Loyalty programs

    Upgrade traditional loyalty points programs to a Web3 experience. Enable instant liquidity, programmable money and new forms of financial benefits. Offer virtual goods, utility NFTs, unique digital content or access to private communities by token holders.

    · Launch your own branded token that customers can earn for good behaviors, initiating purchases or transferring existing loyalty points.

    · Provide liquidity for customers to be able to instantly liquidate a branded token for a base token or redeem it for products on your e-commerce platform.

    · Offer virtual goods and experiences, such as NFTs, unique digital content or access to private communities to holders of your branded token.

  • Social Apps

    Evolve your social community into a healthy economy by enabling a token to drive engagement, reward ideal behaviors, and align incentives with content creators.

    · Launch a branded token, and/or sub-tokens to provide personalized experiences for your social app, fostering brand loyalty.

    · Use your token to offer perks such as products, services or unique experiences for token holders, driving additional engagement.

    · Unlike traditional social and community apps, you can integrate a token, enabling contributing community members to accrue tokens that have value.
